🛡️ Prevention Checklist (Stay Safe Online)
Always verify the sender before clicking links
Do not share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone
Check website URLs carefully before entering details
Avoid clicking on suspicious links or attachments
Use strong and unique passwords
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
Regularly update apps and software
💰 UPI Safety Tips
Never approve unknown payment requests
Double-check UPI IDs before sending money
📢 Reporting
Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930
